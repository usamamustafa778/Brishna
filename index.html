ABOUT US

Who we are

Brishna Global is one of Islamabad’s largest construction company, started off as real estate developers and consultants. Since its inception, it provided its customers with quality services on very economical and market competitive rates. Brishna is known by its expertise in real estate development and consultancy. We have experience of retail shopping center, hospitals, restaurants, luxury apartments, management, and renovation of all kinds. The firm was founded by Shahid Ahmed Khan, Managing Director, in mid-1990’s.


What we deliver

We offer complete project management and coordination of all designs, costs, time, construction, infrastructure, and other property development related elements. We deal in commercial, community building and the residential projects and our team is fully equipped. We offer quality control of all materials used and ensure that the building standards are strictly followed at every step of the building process.
