Suhail Abbasi, in his 41 years long professional history, introduced in his architectural practice, a high degree of rationalization in functional planning as well as innovative aesthetic configurations as the principal in his highly successful architectural, planning & interior design consultancy firm, Suhail and Fawad Architects with his son Fawad Suhail Abbasi.

Icon Architecture & Design

Icon is an award-winning international architecture planning & interior design firm. Icon is comprised of reputed and experienced architects, planners & engineers with a standing of several years in the profession. The firm has designed and supervised various buildings and complexes ranging from residential units to projects in communities, recreation, education, aviation & aerospace, infrastructure, industrial and public health sectors. Besides the planning assignments, ICON has also undertaken outline development, plans, structure plans and master/site plans of various housing schemes and analysis of urban/regional development in different contexts. In this regard the clients have been the government/ semi government departments, public sector agencies and international organizations.
The scope of consultancy services encompasses the disciplines of architecture, interior design, historic preservations, environmental / sustainable design, urban planning, structural & MEP design, construction / project management for a variety of projects.
ICON has collaborated with various national and international consultant groups for providing specialized services in professional fields.
